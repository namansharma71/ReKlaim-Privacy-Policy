ReKlaim Privacy Policy

At ReKlaim, we are committed to protexcting the privacy and security of our users, especially new mothers navigating postpartum fitness. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the ReKlaim app.
Last updated: May 22, 2025

1. Data Collection

To provide personalized postpartum fitness support, we collect the following information:

Personal Information:

  • Name & Age: Required for account creation and tailoring fitness content.
  • Postpartum Timeframe: Helps personalize your fitness plan based on recovery stage.
  • Delivery Type: Used to tailor fitness plans based on whether the user had a vaginal delivery or C-section, ensuring appropriate exercises and recovery pace.
  • Allergies: Used to customize diet or workout recommendations and prevent exposure to unsuitable suggestions.
  • Height & Weight: Essential for fitness goal tracking and BMI calculation.
  • Fitness Goals: To personalize workouts and track progress.
  • Fitness Level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced): Used to recommend appropriate workout plans.

Media Access:

  • Profile Picture: You may upload a photo to personalize your profile.

Location Information:

  • Device Location: With your permission, we use this to help you discover nearby users for motivation and support.

Usage Data:

  • Workout history, goal progression, preferences, and in-app activity used to improve and personalize your fitness journey.

ReKlaim does not use cookies, third-party trackers, or advertising networks.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Account Setup & Authentication: Using your name and age.
  • Personalization: Postpartum stage, fitness level, and physical data allow us to deliver workouts tailored to your unique needs.
  • Profile Customization: Profile picture upload enables identity personalization.
  • Progress Tracking: Height, weight, and fitness goals allow for real-time goal tracking and motivational insights.
  • Social Features: Device location is used (with your consent) to connect you with nearby users for support and community building.

We never use your data for advertising or purposes unrelated to ReKlaim’s functionality.

3. Data Security Measures

  • Encryption: All personal data is encrypted in transit and at rest using modern security protocols.
  • Secure Storage: Information is securely stored usingSupabase with row-level security to restrict access.
  • Minimal Access: Sensitive health-related data is only used internally to deliver relevant features and never shared without your permission.

4. Use of Device Capabilities

  • Camera & Photo Library: To upload and set a profile picture.
  • Location: Used (with your consent) to find and connect with nearby users.

You can manage these permissions in your device settings at any time.

5. Data Sharing

  • Within ReKlaim: Your profile will be shared with nearby users for community feature.
  • Service Providers: We use Supabase for secure storage. These services do not use your data beyond what’s required for ReKlaim’s functionality.

6. Compliance

ReKlaim complies with all applicable data protection laws and regulations in India, including:

  • DPDP Act (Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023): We ensure lawful and transparent processing of personal data, protect user rights, and follow data minimization and storage limitation principles.

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

  • Update: Modify your personal information in the profile section of the app.
  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data by contacting us.
  • Deletion: Request account deletion and removal of all personal data by emailing us.
